the cafè

Come visit OrganicME Cafè, our tasty dishes are made from scratch with 100% organic ingredients.
Our chefs and baristas have been trained in the use of organic ingredients and not one part of our products goes to waste.
Visit us for a fully organic experience, we are opened every day for breakfast and lunch.

Our specialties

Organic, local and sustainable produce will feature across the menu, changing slightly depending on season and what our local farms and suppliers have available.
Check out our menu Here.

our ingredients

We source our food responsibly, supporting small local food producers and suppliers. All of our ingredients are delivered fresh every day. Our amazing coffee is roasted by Dear Green Coffee. Our organic soft drink are produced by Onewater.
