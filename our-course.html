our courses

At OrganicMe we provide gardening courses for everyone! Come learn how to grow your own organic vegetable garden, wether you are a beginner, or already know your way around the dirt.

Time to get your hands dirty

The morning is a classroom lecture, and the afternoon is a practical demonstration within our garden plot to allow you "hands on" experience. We will cover all aspects of what you should be planting, soil conditions, seeds so that you understand the life cycle and learn to grow your own successfully. Dress for the weather on the day but please wear stout boots or "wellies” and a packed lunch.
The price per person is £30.00. Please enquire below.
